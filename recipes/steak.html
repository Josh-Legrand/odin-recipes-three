steak

Ingredients:

  • Steak
  • butter
  • salt
  • peper
  • oil

Step-by-step

  1. Prep the steaks. Remove the steaks from the refrigerator about 30 minutes to 1 hour before cooking.
  2. Prepare the rest of the ingredients.
  3. Season the steaks
  4. Heat the pan
  5. cook the steaks
  6. Flavor and baste the steaks
  7. Rest the steaks (optional)
  8. Carve the steak
  9. enjoy!
