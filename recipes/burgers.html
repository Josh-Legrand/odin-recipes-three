American Burgers

Learn how to make and build one of American's most mouth watering, flavor-building, sawwed after sandwhichs known to A-man-ericans.

Ingrediantes needed for this meal

  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Ground beef
  • Burger bread
  • Butter

Optional additions

  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Tomatos
  • Onions
  • Cheese
    • American
    • Provolone
    • Chedder
    • Pepper Jack
    • Blue
  • Bacon
    • pork
    • bacon
  • Lettuce

Steps-by-step:

  1. Turn on heat to a nice medium-low
  2. Pour 1/4 of Olive oil on pan
  3. Place a well portioned slab of beef in the shape of a circle,center in the fire.
  4. Use salt and seasoning on both sides of patty
  5. Use another pan to heat up and cook buns
  6. Place butter on pan prior to place down bun
  7. Once bun and patty are cooked to your liking begin building your burger with additional options if applicable.
  8. enjoy!
